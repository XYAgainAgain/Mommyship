# MOMMYSHIP
**A Homebrew Table Ruleset** for **Mothership Sci-Fi Horror RPG**

---

Welcome to Mommyship! This is a mildly friendlier, more robust, and ever-so-slightly less deadly take on the [Mothership RPG](https://www.tuesdayknightgames.com/pages/mothership-rpg). This site contains the complete homebrew ruleset, split into 3 parts:

- **[Player's Survival Guide](players-survival-guide/index.md):** Character creation, stats, skills, classes, species, combat, & gear.
- **[Shipbreaker's Toolkit](shipbreakers-toolkit/index.md):** Ships, space travel, operating costs, & ship combat.
- **[D.A.V.E. Pilot Manual](dave-pilot-manual.md):** Design & pilot your very own **D**efense **A**nd **V**iolent **E**ncounter droid.

Mommyship was created by two big nerds, **Jaz** & **Sam**, to fill some gaps in the original system.

---

*Mommyship is a horror game for mature audiences. It contains violence, foul language, body horror, some sexual content, drug use, and depictions of mental illness, trauma, stress, panic, and capitalistic abuse which may not be suitable for all audiences. Please be advised.*
