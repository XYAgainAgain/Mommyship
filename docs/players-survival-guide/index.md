# MOMMYSHIP

## **PLAYER'S SURVIVAL GUIDE**

***SYSTEM CHANGES***

**Mommyship** differs from [**Mothership**](https://www.tuesdayknightgames.com/pages/mothership-rpg)**®** in several notable ways — changing Stats and how they are used, adding additional Class options, completely new Species, and a handful of additional Skills geared towards social encounters, psychic aptitude, and sick sci-fi kickflips — as well as new ways to learn additional Skills. Patches now offer mechanical benefits which can pass over from one character to another when one meets an untimely demise, giving a non-linear progression as you continue to play.

In addition, Armor is completely reworked to be more useful and longer lasting; Weapon options are greatly expanded and differentiated to encourage exciting and unique combat playstyles; Stimpacks have gained some siblings in our new Chem list. Contractors have been reworked to further simplify their mechanics, and allow for a modular approach to designing new kinds of Contractors from various backgrounds and walks of life.

Last but not least, an entirely new Character Sheet has been designed to align with all these changes.
